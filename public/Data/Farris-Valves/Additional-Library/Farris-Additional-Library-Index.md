# Farris Engineering - Additional Library PDFs

Source: https://valves.curtisswright.com/en-us/farris/library

Supplemental set of 43 Farris Engineering documents gathered from the manufacturer's
Library section to expand the original Farris-Valves package (which only had 2 InSure
PDFs). Organized by category below.

## Series-Catalogs (10) - full product catalogs per series
## Installation-Maintenance-Manuals (7) - IOM instructions per series
## iPRSM (5) - Intelligent Pressure Relief System Management program materials
## Technical-Articles-Compliance (6) - third-party articles, PSM/bellows/compliance
## Brochures (6) - industry & product overview brochures (incl. Dyna-Flo overview)
## Overview-Brochures-Multilingual (9) - Farris overview brochure in 9 languages

Best candidates for a public "Resources" download page on the ACTS site (per the
website design notes in the uploaded ACTS content doc):
- CW-ProductOverviewBrochure (Farris) and CW-DF-ProductOverviewBrochure (Dyna-Flo)
- Series catalogs matching each "Relevant Product Line" on the Industries We Serve page
  (1890, 2600, 2700, 3800, 4200, 6400/6600, 1896)
- InSure IOMM (newer 10-2025 R4 revision) + InSure Catalog
- iPRSM brochure (supports the "aftermarket accountability" story)

Technical articles, compliance PDFs, and multilingual brochures are better suited as
internal reference material than public downloads.
