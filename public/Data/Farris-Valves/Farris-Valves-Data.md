## Overview
Farris Engineering provides pressure, flow control management, heat exchanger repair and isolation products to industrial markets: pressure relief valves, directional control valves, the inSure real-time monitoring device, SizeMaster sizing software, and aftermarket services.

## Directional Control Valves
- Allow fluids/gases to flow into different paths from valve ports; mechanically/electrically actuated spool inside a cylinder
- Features: Power beyond, Blocked and Open spool options; 4W3P Manually Operated; Lightweight aluminum construction; Integral relief in P; High Flow option (15 GPM); 2–21 sections; SAE #8 ports A & B; SAE #10 ports P & T; Custom porting; 12 gpm, 2200 psi intermittent
- Applications: Agricultural, Industrial, Mobile — car carriers, tractors, assembly lines, energy production, marine equipment, construction/mining machines, material handling cranes, trucks & rigs
