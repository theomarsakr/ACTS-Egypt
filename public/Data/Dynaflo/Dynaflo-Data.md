## Overview
A business unit of Curtiss-Wright Valves Division. With over 30 years' experience, Dyna-Flo provides control valve systems, technology, and engineering that enables customers to perform efficiently and achieve safe, reliable plant operation while reducing total cost of ownership.

Industries served: Upstream/Midstream/Downstream Oil & Gas, Chemical/Petrochemical, Mining, Pulp and Paper, Power Generation, Food and Beverage, General Industry, Agriculture and Construction. Also supports clean/renewable energy: LNG, Hydrogen, Biofuel.

Featured products: New DF400 Eccentric Rotary Plug Valve and Actuator control system; 320 AxFlo Anti-Cavitation heavy-duty multi-stage axial flow valve.

### Rotary Plug Valves — DF400 Series
Heavy-duty eccentric plug control valve (rotary globe valve) combined with a low-profile spring-and-diaphragm actuator. Used in oil & gas production, chemical process industries, and severe service environments. Self-aligning eccentric plug with straight-through flow pattern provides tight shutoff with higher flow capacities.
- 1 to 6 inch NPS (25-150mm DN), flanged body, quarter-turn operation, RF end connection
- ASME B16.34 Class 150-600 pressure rating; ANSI/FCI 70.2 & IEC 60534-4 Class IV/VI shutoff
- Options: standard low-emission packing, blowout proof shafts, reduced port trim, NACE construction, high-temp/severe-service coatings
- Body materials: WCC, CF3M; Shutoff Class IV (metal seat) or Class VI (PTFE soft seat)

## Actuators

### DFC & DFO Linear Spring & Diaphragm Actuators
Large diaphragm area actuators for low-pressure operation. Spring provides fail-safe positioning on loss of pneumatic supply. DFC (Fail Closed) and DFO (Fail Open) automate control valves for throttling or on/off control of liquids or gases.
- Input signal: 3-15 PSIG (0.21-1.03 BARG) or 6-30 PSIG (0.41-2.07 BARG)
- Valve stem compatibility: 3/8", 1/2", 3/4"; Yoke boss: 2-1/8", 2-13/16", 3-9/16"
- Temperature: -40°F to 180°F (-40°C to 82°C)
- Sizes: 1046, 1069, 2069, 2105, 2156, 3105, 3156, 3220 (nominal effective area 46-220 in²)
- Options: open yoke/open valve stem design, heavy duty coatings, handwheels/travel stops, versatile instrument mounting

### DFLP Linear Pneumatic Piston Actuator

- Minimum cylinder operating pressure: 20 PSIG (1.38 BARG)
- Valve stem compatibility: 3/4", 1", 1-1/4"; Yoke boss: 3-9/16" or 5"
- Temperature: -40°F to 180°F (-40°C to 82°C)
- Sizes: 3113, 4113, 5113, 4154, 5154 (thrust up to ~16,940 lbf)
